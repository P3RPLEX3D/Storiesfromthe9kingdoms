This Means War!

A story about a little girl

When 2 war-ring nations attacked

Her father told her to hide

So she ran into the nearby forest

The village was over-run, and her family murdered.

The little girl waited but her father never came.

The forest soon became her home

Years past and as time went on the little girl grew

One day the drums of war echoed through the forest once again

This time however, she did not run.

She was no longer a little girl.

The forrest had spoken, and fate had made its choice. She was no longer a little girl.

she was in fact a Bear, With little paws.
